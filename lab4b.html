Interesting Facts

1. India is known for its diversity. The largest democracy in the world.
2. The Indian flag is tricolor. The national flag was adopted on July 22, 1947.
3. A land of a billion dreams. It has a population of over a billion people.
4. A symbol of love: The Taj Mahal. Built by Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.
5. A linguistic treasure: Hindi is widely spoken. India has 22 officially recognized languages.
